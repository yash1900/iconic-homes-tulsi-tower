Our Story

A family-owned builder focused on crafting strong foundations and long-term buyer relationships in Navi Mumbai since 2011.

Est. 2011

A Legacy of Personal Supervision

Iconic Homes Infracon was founded by Amar Malhotra with a singular commitment: treating every client's house construction as if it were his own family home.

Before launching Iconic Homes, Amar ji spent 15 years supervising civil construction projects for large infrastructure corporations. He noticed a worrying trend: as developers grew bigger, their designs became more sterile, their materials cheaper, and their relationship with actual homebuyers became completely disconnected, hidden behind brokers and salespeople.

He founded Iconic Homes to change that. By focusing on a single active development at any given time, he ensures that every pillar cast, every brick laid, and every plumbing line run is inspected by him or his direct site managers. There are no middle-men and no brokerage commissions. You deal direct with the builder.

Our Pillars

The Values We Build Upon

A family home is the largest investment most Indian households make. We protect that investment through absolute commitment to three core operational values.

Uncompromising Integrity

We upload our structural maps, layout permissions, and bank escrow files directly to the state RERA portal. We do not make false promises about possession times or material brands. What is written in the contract is exactly what is delivered.

On-Site Supervision

We limit our portfolio to one active construction project at a time. This allows our management team to live on-site, supervising contractors daily to prevent shortcuts in plumbing, waterproofing, and structural steel reinforcement.

Direct Accountability

We do not hire commission-based real estate brokers to sell our houses. Our site office is managed by our own team. If you buy a home from us, you have a direct phone line to the company directors, ensuring any concern is solved instantly.
