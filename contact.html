Frequently Asked Questions

Grounded answers to questions commonly raised by Navimumbai apartment buyers.

Are home loans approved for Tulsi Tower?

Yes, Tulsi Tower is approved for home financing by major nationalized and private banks including State Bank of India (SBI), HDFC Bank, ICICI Bank, and LIC Housing Finance. Our sales office will handle bank documentation files for you.

How can I verify the RERA number of the project?

The project is registered under MahaRERA number P51800098765. You can search this number directly on the official state MahaRERA portal. It shows our layout approvals, promoter escrow records, and construction stage audits.

What are the site visiting hours?

Our site office in Sector 12 Kharghar is open from Monday to Saturday, 10:00 AM to 6:00 PM. We recommend booking in advance using our form so we can arrange parking or a site manager guide.

Do you charge commission or brokerage fees?

No, we do not. Since you buy directly from Iconic Homes (the promoter and builder), there are zero brokerage fees or commission markups. Every transaction follows builder-direct cost structures.
