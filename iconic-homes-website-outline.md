# Iconic Homes Infracon — Website Content Outline

> Research-backed content blueprint for a family-owned Indian real estate and construction developer.
> Based on analysis of Godrej Properties, Mahindra Lifespaces, Lodha Group, and DLF websites (May 2026).

---

## 1. Recommended Page Structure

| Page | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| **Home** | First impression — communicate brand, trust, and active project at a glance |
| **About Us** | Tell the family story, founding values, and what makes Iconic Homes different from a broker |
| **Projects → Tulsi Tower** | Dedicated project page with full specs, gallery, RERA number, and lead form |
| **Projects → Completed** | Portfolio of delivered work — strongest trust signal for a growing developer |
| **Why Choose Us** | Explicit differentiators: quality construction, on-time delivery, transparent pricing |
| **Gallery** | High-quality photos and site-progress images (construction milestones) |
| **Blog / News** | Optional — construction updates, real estate tips, local area guides (aids SEO) |
| **Contact Us** | Phone, WhatsApp, office address with Google Maps embed, and inquiry form |
| **RERA Disclosures** | Legal compliance page — RERA registration numbers, six-monthly progress reports |

## 2. Homepage Blueprint

Sections in order, with suggested copy angle:

### Section 1 — Hero (Full-width visual)
- **Visual:** Render or hero shot of Tulsi Tower (or aspirational lifestyle shot)
- **Headline angle:** Strength + warmth — "Built on Trust. Designed for Life."
- **Sub-copy:** "Iconic Homes Infracon — crafting quality homes in [City] since [Year]."
- **CTAs:** "Explore Tulsi Tower" (primary) · "Call Us Now" (secondary)
- **Note:** Hero should load fast on mobile (majority of Indian buyers browse on phones)

### Section 2 — Stats Bar (Social proof at a glance)
- Years in business | Homes delivered | Happy families | Projects under construction
- Example: "15+ Years · 200+ Homes · 1 Active Project · 100% RERA Compliant"

### Section 3 — Active Project Spotlight (Tulsi Tower)
- Project name, location, BHK types available, possession date
- 2-3 key highlights (e.g., "Vastu-compliant layouts · Modern amenities · Prime location")
- CTA: "View Full Details"

### Section 4 — About the Company
- 3-4 sentences on the family behind the brand (Amar Malhotra as majority partner)
- Emphasise: local expertise, personal involvement, builder-direct pricing (no broker markup)
- CTA: "Our Story"

### Section 5 — Why Iconic Homes (Differentiators)
- 3 icon cards: **Quality Construction** · **Timely Delivery** · **Transparent Dealing**
- One-liners under each: e.g., "We use [brand] cement and grade steel — no shortcuts."

### Section 6 — Completed Projects / Portfolio
- 3–4 thumbnail cards of past delivered projects
- Reinforces: "We finish what we start" — key anxiety for Indian off-plan buyers

### Section 7 — Testimonials
- 2–3 real buyer quotes with name, project name, and year
- WhatsApp-style review screenshots perform well on Indian audiences

### Section 8 — Construction Progress (if Tulsi Tower is under construction)
- Photo strip of site progress with date stamps
- Trust signal: shows the project is real and moving

### Section 9 — Contact / Inquiry Strip
- Phone number (click-to-call), WhatsApp button, and quick lead form
- "Visit our site office at [address] — Mon to Sat, 10am to 6pm"

## 3. Tulsi Tower Project Page

Must-have sections:

### Overview
- Project name, location (neighbourhood + city), RERA registration number (mandatory)
- Project type: Residential / Mixed Use
- Status: Under Construction / Ready to Move
- Expected possession: [Month, Year]

### Configuration & Pricing
- BHK types offered (e.g., 2 BHK, 3 BHK)
- Carpet area / built-up area range (in sq ft and sq mt)
- Price range: Starting at ₹X lakhs (or "Price on Request")
- EMI calculator widget (very high engagement in Indian market)

### Amenities
- Bulleted list with icons: Lift, Parking, Power Backup, Security, Play Area, Club House, etc.
- Vastu compliance note if applicable

### Floor Plans
- Downloadable PDF floor plans per BHK type
- Interactive floor plan viewer if budget allows

### Gallery
- Construction progress photos with dates
- Renders / 3D walkthroughs
- Location map (Google Maps embed + nearby landmarks: school, hospital, highway)

### RERA Details
- RERA registration number with link to state RERA portal
- Promoter details as per RERA
- Six-monthly progress reports (legally required, also builds trust)

### Lead Capture
- Sticky "Enquire Now" button on mobile
- Form: Name, Phone, BHK preference, Budget range, Possession timeline
- WhatsApp enquiry button (highest conversion channel in India)

## 4. Trust Signals

Priority trust signals for an Indian developer website:

| Signal | Why It Matters |
|--------|---------------|
| **RERA Registration Number** | Legally mandatory; buyers actively verify on state RERA portal |
| **Years in business** | Longevity = reliability; "Est. [Year]" in header/footer |
| **Completed projects with photos** | Proof of delivery; counters "will they actually finish?" fear |
| **Builder-direct badge** | "Buy direct from developer — no brokerage" drives enquiries |
| **Family ownership story** | Personal accountability; differentiates from faceless corporates |
| **Construction quality specs** | Brand of cement/steel/fittings used; appeals to quality-conscious buyers |
| **Buyer testimonials** | With full name and project — anonymous reviews are discounted |
| **ISO / BIS certifications** (if any) | Godrej Properties uses ISO as a headline trust signal |
| **Bank approvals** | "Home loans approved by SBI, HDFC, ICICI" — removes financing friction |
| **Awards / press mentions** | Local builder awards, newspaper coverage (even local paper counts) |
| **Legal & compliance disclosures** | Visible disclaimer per RERA norms builds confidence in serious buyers |

## 5. Copy Tone Guide

**Voice in 4 words:** Warm · Dependable · Grounded · Aspirational

The tone should feel like a trusted family elder giving advice — not a salesperson. Avoid superlatives like "world-class" or "luxurious" unless they are backed by specifics. Use plain language, not real estate jargon.

**Example headline:** "Your family deserves a home built the way ours would build it."

**Additional examples:**
- About section: "Amar Malhotra started Iconic Homes with one belief: a home is the most important purchase of a family's life. We treat it that way."
- Project tagline: "Tulsi Tower — Where every corner is crafted with care."
- Testimonial intro: "Hear from families who call our buildings home."

**What to avoid:** "Ultra-luxury", "World-class amenities", "Dream home awaits" (overused; erodes trust)

## 6. Indian Market Notes

### RERA Compliance (Non-Negotiable)
- Display RERA number prominently on every project page — above the fold
- Link directly to the state RERA portal for the project
- Upload six-monthly progress reports as legally required
- Failure to display RERA details creates immediate buyer distrust and legal risk

### WhatsApp as Primary Lead Channel
- Indian buyers prefer WhatsApp over phone calls for initial enquiry
- Add a WhatsApp floating button on every page (wa.me link)
- Response time matters: set up a WhatsApp Business account with auto-reply

### EMI Calculator
- A built-in EMI calculator ("Pay just ₹X/month") significantly increases time-on-site and conversions
- Pre-fill with SBI home loan rate (most common reference point)

### Vastu Shastra
- Call out Vastu compliance explicitly if layouts follow it — it is a genuine purchase driver for a significant segment of Indian buyers, especially in Tier 2/3 cities
- Even a single line — "Vastu-compliant floor plans available" — impacts decisions

### Mobile-First Design
- Over 70% of Indian real estate browsing is on mobile; ensure site loads in under 3 seconds on 4G
- Click-to-call buttons must be above the fold on mobile

### Language
- Hindi or local regional language toggle adds trust in Tier 2 markets
- Even just the tagline in Hindi ("आपके सपनों का घर") alongside English signals local connection

### Broker vs. Developer Differentiation
- Brokers list many developers' projects and earn commission — buyers know this
- Developer sites should lean into "builder-direct" as a pricing and accountability advantage
- Feature the construction team, the site engineer, the founder — real people = real accountability

### Photography Timing
- Avoid showing empty plots. Use architectural renders early, real construction photos as work progresses
- Progress photos with dates are far more convincing than renders for under-construction projects
