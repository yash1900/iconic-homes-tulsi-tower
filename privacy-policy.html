Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Learn how we handle your personal data when you submit booking enquiries on our website.

1. Data We Collect

When you fill out contact forms or booking inquiry forms on this website, we collect your Name, Phone Number, Email Address, and your stated apartment configurations preferences. We do not share this data with third-party advertising companies.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your contact details solely to send floor plans, structural layouts, and pricing quotations directly to you. Our site office manager may use your phone number to coordinate taxi pickups or schedule on-site walkthroughs. We do not run automated cold calling campaigns.

3. WhatsApp Communications

If you click on our WhatsApp floating widget, you initiate a chat with our WhatsApp Business manager. Standard WhatsApp end-to-end encryption applies to those conversations. You can opt-out or block the business account at any time.

4. Security Standards

Your data is stored in secure databases accessed only by authorized sales managers of Iconic Homes Infracon. Sourced database sheets are audited monthly to ensure data is protected and removed when no longer required.

5. Contact Details

If you wish to request the deletion of your submitted contact records from our database, please email enquiry@iconichomes.in or walk into our site office at Kharghar.
