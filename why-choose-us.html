Why Choose Us

We believe in specific standards, not vague sales slogans. Read details of our materials, funds escrow protection, and direct pricing advantages.

Uncompromising Construction Quality

We do not hide behind plaster. We source certified, branded raw materials to ensure the structural strength and insulation of your flat remains flawless for decades.

Cement Grade: UltraTech / Ambuja Portland Pozzolana Cement (PPC) for structural pillars and slabs.
Reinforcement Steel: TATA Tiscon TMT Fe-550D steel bars for high earthquake-resistant safety.
Electrical Cabling: Finolex / Polycab flame-retardant low-smoke (FR-LS) copper wiring.
Plumbing Pipes: Astral / Supreme CPVC pipes to guarantee zero leakage and rust.
Paint & Finishes: Asian Paints Royale interior plastic emulsion and Apex Ultima weather-proof exterior coat.
Bathroom Fittings: Jaquar / Kohler dual-flush sanitaryware and brass chromium-plated fittings.

Our Timely Possession Promise

Indian off-plan buyers worry most about project delays. We eliminate this anxiety using strict financial and project tracking systems.

  • RERA Escrow Protection: Under RERA compliance guidelines, 70% of all client booking amounts are stored in a locked project escrow bank account. These funds can only be withdrawn for construction costs, meaning work never halts due to other company investments.
  • One Active Project Focus: By building only one project at a time, our entire crew, site engineer, and director team are focused on completing Tulsi Tower on schedule.
  • Milestone-Linked Payments: Your payments are strictly linked to verified structural completions (e.g., foundation casting, slab completion). If construction stops, your payments stop automatically.

Zero Broker Markups, Direct Pricing

We sell directly to you. This simple change removes broker fees and ensures direct accountability.

  • No Broker Fees: When you buy through third-party agents, 2% to 4% of your money goes to commission. Sourcing directly from us eliminates this cost, which is passed on to you in our pricing.
  • No Hidden Costs: We detail all charges—such as development fees, electric meter connection charges, and club costs—up front before you pay a single booking rupee. There are no sudden pricing demands at possession.
  • Direct Access to Management: If you have a query about your payments, interest rates, structural changes, or registration dates, you don't call an agent. You walk into our site office and talk directly to Rahul or Amar Malhotra.
